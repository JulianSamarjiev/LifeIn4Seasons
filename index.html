“Life in Four Seasons” is a fascinating story in which our hero, a six year old boy has to face many obstacles to save the girl he loves.
To gather the strength he'll need to face the challenges, he goes through a series of illusionary adventures from his favourite books, travelling through Bulgaria in past and present. Along his way, he is guided by legendary Bulgarians, that help him overcome the hurdles that he faces, build self belief and uncover the power of his inner courage! Our story has lots of twists, follow us on this journey to find out how it ends!

The film will be dubbed in 8 languages (Bulgarian, English, Spanish, Mandarin, Japanese, Russian, German and Korean) and we believe to reach hundreds of millions of people around the globe, every child, or grown up that can relate to our hero.

The short film will be not for profit and aims to motivate people to rediscover their roots, build more authentic relationships and follow their dreams. Another meaningful aspect of the film is to showcase Bulgaria, it’s nature, history and spirit.
